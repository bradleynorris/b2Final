about

I am Bradley C. Norris and I am a student attempting to learn all I can about website development. I have a passon for designing good looking websites that stems from my backround of always being involved in artistic persuits. Below you'll find just a few of my drawings and doodles. These are just a few that I have pulled from the multitude of notebooks I have filled with drawings, sketches and doodles. Any spare time I have is usually spent drawing in these notebooks. I'm looking to take my artistic eye and apply it to web development; creating websites that are as beautiful as they are functional. I hope to add some more professional websites to the list below in the near future. I have way too many hobbies but I try to manage them all well. I love to hike, backpack and get outdoors as much as possible. I love all things soccer. I play street and indoor soccer and you can typically find me up drinking coffee at 4 a.m. on the weekends to catch a Premier League match. I love to fish and am an avid cyclist. Lets just say, I stay busy. Finding time in my schedule to code has been a challenge, but a welcomed addition to my slate of interests. If you have any questions or would like to just reach out and chat, feel free to use the contact form below.
